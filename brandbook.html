The Essence

The Secret Dwells Within

Apparel that asks the questions civilization forgot to ask. For the spiritually curious and the cosmically playful, DMT World translates the language of inner space into wearable art — sparking conversations, questions, and awakenings one graphic tee at a time. Rooted in genuine inquiry, served with humor, beauty, and a wink from a machine elf.

Personality

The Cosmic Trickster

Jester + Sage. The coyote of consciousness — holding the deepest ideas with the lightest hands.

Voice

Cosmic · Witty · Warm

Depth without solemnity, humor without shallowness. Every line could've been texted to a friend at 2 a.m. by someone very amused and very awake.

Promise

Awaken, Don't Preach

We celebrate the questions, never claim the answers. Wordplay that rewards attention; warmth that includes everyone in the joke.

Guardrail

Art, Not Instruction

Expressive art about consciousness, spirituality, and the psychedelic imagination. Never supply, sourcing, dosage, or facilitation. We create; you contemplate.
