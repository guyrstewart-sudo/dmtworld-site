One Big Cosmic Joke

And you're invited to be in on it.

DMT World started with a simple, slightly mischievous idea: that a t-shirt could be a doorway. Not to anywhere you have to go — but to a question you get to ask. A stranger reads your chest, tilts their head, and for one second the autopilot switches off. That second is the whole point.

Since 2014, from a corner of Asheville, North Carolina, we've been screen-printing that second onto cotton. More than ten thousand of them have shipped around the planet, earning a 4.8-star reputation and a 471,000-strong family who treat the feed like a temple with a great sense of humor. We are equal parts art studio, comedy act, and consciousness experiment — and we refuse to pick just one.

The Secret Dwells Within

That's our north star, lifted straight from the original mission: to promote a conscious awakening through the use of clothing. The designs are built to spark questions from others and create an awareness that nudges people — gently, absurdly — toward waking up to how strange and beautiful it all is. We're not here to tell you what's true. We're here to make you laugh hard enough that you go looking for yourself.

Why the machine elves

Terence McKenna described meeting "self-transforming machine elves" in the deepest reaches of imagination — tricksters that sing reality into being and wink at you while they do it. That image runs through everything we make: the humor, the sacred geometry, the sense that the universe is both profoundly meaningful and absolutely kidding around. Our mascot winks for a reason.

"You are all divine beings."
